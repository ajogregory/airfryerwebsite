Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is an air fryer?
An air fryer is a kitchen appliance that cooks by circulating hot air around the food. It produces a crispy layer similar to frying but uses much less oil.
2. How does an air fryer work?
Air fryers use convection to circulate hot air and cook food. A heating element generates heat, and a fan circulates the hot air, cooking the food from all angles.
3. What can I cook in an air fryer?
You can cook a variety of foods, including fries, chicken wings, vegetables, and even baked goods like muffins and cakes.
4. Is cooking with an air fryer healthy?
Cooking with an air fryer is generally healthier than deep frying because it uses significantly less oil. It can reduce the calorie and fat content of your meals.
5. How do I clean my Nirvana air fryer?
To clean your Nirvana air fryer, remove the basket and pan and wash them with warm soapy water. Use a damp cloth to wipe the inside and outside of the appliance.
